About YUNAYY

Innovating Community Mobility Through Technology

Who We Are

Yunayy is a technology-driven company dedicated to revolutionizing the way people and goods move across India. Our mission is to create solutions that are smart, safe, and community-powered. We're proud to introduce our flagship mobile application — AASHU.

Our Product – AASHU

AASHU is a next-gen mobile app that brings together ride-sharing, parcel delivery, and service booking — all in one platform. Designed to empower individuals and optimize empty vehicle movement, AASHU fosters community collaboration and reduces carbon footprint.

Our Mission

To connect people and packages efficiently using community resources and smart routing.

Our Vision

To become India’s most trusted platform for integrated transport, logistics, and booking solutions.

Our Values

Transparency, Safety, Innovation, Affordability, and Sustainability.
