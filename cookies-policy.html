Cookies Policy

How we use cookies on our website and app

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to enhance your experience. They help us understand how you use our site and improve performance and security.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core functionality (e.g., login, navigation).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help track usage, detect issues, and improve the user experience.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember preferences and settings for a smoother experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: Help deliver relevant ads through third-party services (only if applicable).

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use trusted third-party services like Google Analytics to understand user behavior and traffic. These services may use cookies in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling essential cookies may impact site functionality.

5. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. Updates will be published on this page.
